                and engineering programs at the University of Alaska Anchorage (UAA).

                the largest single corporate gift that the University system,
                including UAA, has received.

                fund equipment for the state-of-the-art ConocoPhillips Integrated
                Science Building which is due to open its doors in fall of 2009; $11 million
